Studio Dilettant is a construction studio, based in Berlin. The studio is specialized in creating custom-made solutions for institutions and individuals.

Our approach is achieved through research and analytics. Good results are created through the first ideas — via discussion and concept — til the last details of the production.

We focus on projects within the fields of interior design and architecture. The technical feasibility of your concept is tested by us before we produce it, using the appropriate manufacturing processes and surface treatments. A personal consultant will guide your project from start to finished product.

Studio Dilettant is run by Maxim Lachmann and Marcello Kaiser.
